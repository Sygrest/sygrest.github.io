# My Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio website! This is where I showcase my professional experience, skills, and projects.

## How to Customize Your Site

### 1. **Edit Your Information**
Open `index.html` and replace these placeholders with YOUR information:

- **John Arnold** - Your full name (appears in top navigation and footer)
- **"Welcome to My Portfolio"** - Your personal headline
- **"I'm a professional..."** - Your tagline
- **About Me section** - Write 2-3 sentences about yourself
- **Experience section** - Add your job titles, companies, and achievements
- **Skills section** - List your technical skills, soft skills, tools, and languages
- **Contact section** - Replace with your real email, LinkedIn, and GitHub links

### 2. **Customize the Colors (Optional)**
If you want to change the purple gradient colors:
1. Open `style.css`
2. Find all instances of `#667eea` (light purple) and `#764ba2` (dark purple)
3. Replace with your preferred colors

### 3. **Publish Your Site**
Your site will automatically appear at:
```
https://sygrest.github.io/
```
(Wait 1-2 minutes after committing changes)

## File Structure

- **index.html** - Your main website page
- **style.css** - The styling/design of your site
- **README.md** - This file

## Tips for Writing Your Resume

✅ **Experience**: Focus on accomplishments, not just duties
- Example: "Increased sales by 25%" (better than "Handled sales")

✅ **Skills**: List technologies, languages, and soft skills relevant to your industry

✅ **Contact**: Include your email, LinkedIn, and GitHub (if applicable)

✅ **Keep it concise**: Employers want to scan it quickly

## Getting Help

If you need to make changes:
1. Go to `index.html` on GitHub
2. Click the pencil icon to edit
3. Make your changes and save

No coding knowledge needed - you're just editing text!
